Favorite Book: Dune

Amazon Listing

I credit this book with beginning my interest in reading for fun, fiction in general and science fiction in particular. I love the deep world building and setting of the universe of Dune, and the intermixing of religion/philosophy throughout the work challenging our assumptions about centralizing power and following charismatic figures in our world. As I've reread the work, it is also remarkable that the author Frank Herbert packaged such dense material in an enjoyable and sound narrative structure.
